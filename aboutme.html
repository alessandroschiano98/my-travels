About Me

Traveling, to me, is much more than moving from one place to another. It’s a way to breathe in new perspectives, to rediscover myself among the streets of distant cities and the silence of unfamiliar landscapes. Even when life brings obstacles, limits, or moments when traveling seems impossible, there’s always a spark inside me pushing to explore — to chase that feeling of freedom only a journey can bring. I can’t always do it as much as I’d like, but every small step, every new destination, is part of my greater goal: to keep discovering the world — and myself — one journey at a time.
